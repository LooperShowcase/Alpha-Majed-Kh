Vinícius Júnior

Vinícius Júnior, also known as "Vini Jr.," is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a winger for Real Madrid and the Brazil national team. Born on July 12, 2000, in São Gonçalo, Brazil, he is known for his incredible pace, dribbling ability, and flair on the field.

Career Highlights:

Early Career: Vinícius began his professional career at Flamengo in Brazil, where he made his debut in 2017. His performances quickly caught the attention of top European clubs. Move to Real Madrid: In 2017, Real Madrid signed Vinícius for a reported fee of €45 million, making him one of the most expensive young players in football history at the time. He officially joined Real Madrid in 2018, after turning 18. Real Madrid Success: At Real Madrid, Vinícius has grown into one of the key players, helping the team win multiple titles, including La Liga and the UEFA Champions League. International Career: Vinícius has represented Brazil at various youth levels and made his senior debut in 2019. He has since become an important part of the national team.

Jude Bellingham

Jude Victor William Bellingham is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for La Liga club Real Madrid and the England national team. He is considered one of the best players in the world

Luka Modrić

Luka Modrić is a Croatian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for and captains both La Liga club Real Madrid and the Croatia national team. He is regarded as one of the greatest midfielders of all time, and as the best Croatian footballer ever.

Eduardo Camavinga

Eduardo Celmi Camavinga is a professional footballer who plays for La Liga club Real Madrid and the France national team. A versatile and technically gifted player, he is mostly known for his tackling and mobility
